Sudoku jungle

School Project

Sudoku jungle is a Leap Motion project realised during my second year at l'Ecole Multimédia. We decided to recreate a sudoku game, were you can grab the number underneath the grid with a pinch of two fingers then drag and drop it where you want on the screen. If you drop it on a box, the value of the box changes into the number you've dropped. When you filled all of the boxes, you click the submit button and find out if you won.

This project was realised with Melissa Benmeziane.
I handled the Design, the HTML/CSS and half of the JavaScript
